# STRAH: Another Horizon -- TIPS full text

This file contains the **complete, full text** of every TIPS encyclopedia entry, as translated for this localization. The in-game TIPS box clips long entries past a fixed number of lines (see `local-scripts/tips_fit.py`); entries shortened in-game are marked there with a trailing `[Summarized - see notes]` -- this file always shows the full original wording regardless.

Entries are listed in their in-game index order (no in-game category data is available outside the game itself).

## #000 -- Shinpei Ajiro

Born May 8, 2000. 18 years old. Blood type O. He was born the eldest son of the Ajiro family of underwater archaeologists, but lost his parents in an accident at age 8. Alain Kofune became his foster father. Influenced by Alain and his daughter Ushio, he discovered a passion for cooking and moved to Tokyo after middle school. He currently attends a culinary school where he can also earn his high school diploma. His specialty is chicken curry. Influenced by his biological father, he has loved books since childhood, particularly mysteries and sci-fi. His favorite author is Ryunosuke Nagumo. He has a habit of taking a step back to view things objectively from a bird's-eye perspective. He is afraid of water and cannot swim.

## #001 -- Appoke

A Wakayama dialect word meaning "idiot" or "dummy." Rather than a harsh insult, it's a somewhat softer expression that is often used playfully between close friends.

## #002 -- Amenonuhoko

A sacred treasure of the Hitogashima Shrine. It shares its name with the spear used to create the land in Japanese mythology. According to the shrine's history, it has been passed down for over several millennia, and it is said that the drops from this spear birthed the island of Onogoro. The spearhead is forged from rust-proof meteoric iron, and while intended for ceremonial use, it possesses high lethal force. The handle portion has been remade numerous times over the years; the current one was crafted from oak during the Edo period.

## #003 -- Alain's Field

A private vegetable garden located on the way from Restaurant Kofune to Mt. Takanosu. Tended to with a meticulous focus on the soil itself, the fresh vegetables harvested here are one of the secrets to Kofune's flavor.

## #004 -- Event Flyer

A secret live concert featuring popular idol and Hitogashima Island native, Kaori Koyuba! There will also be a talk show and a handshake event! Locals and visitors alike are all welcome to join us!

Date/Time: July 24, 2018, starting at 20:00~ • Location: Special Stage on the Hitogashima Shrine Grounds • Sponsors: Hitogashima Chamber of Commerce • Hitogashima Shrine

* Participants for the handshake event will be selected by lottery.

## #005 -- Event Horizon

The observational boundary. It refers to a cliff-like concept that steadily closes in with each loop. It represents the limit of the scope where a do-over is possible on the timeline, creeping up behind Shinpei as time flows forward. The further away this cliff is, the more time he has to try again; the closer it gets, the less leeway he has left. If this cliff catches up to him and he falls, Shinpei's death will be finalized, and from then on, the timeline observed by Haine will become cemented as objective reality.

## #006 -- Ushio's Present ①

A present given to him by Ushio in a dream. However, he doesn't know what it is yet.

## #007 -- Ushio's Present ②

14 years ago, the shock of eating Ryunosuke caused Haine to panic, and her persona as the human girl Hoine separated from Hiruko's body. The mud comprising the right eye she took then drifted in the sea for years, eventually copying Ushio in a chance encounter. After maturing and awakening as the Observer's right eye, it transcended time to be handed over to Shinpei. It marked the end of everything, and the beginning as well.

## #008 -- Ushio's Shadow ①

July 18th. A beach cleanup drive was held at Omoto Beach, the coastline spreading out in front of Restaurant Kofune. It was an event to collect trash in preparation for a local Hitogashima Island ritual. While participating, Ushio and Mio spotted a young girl standing on the rocks. Her appearance was purportedly a dead ringer for Ushio.

## #009 -- Ushio's Shadow ②

July 24th. The Summer Festival, Hitogashima Island's biggest event, was held. Catching a glimpse of a woman who looked like Ushio, Shinpei left Mio and the others behind and instinctively ran after her. Though he lost sight of her in the crowd, after making his way to Omoto Beach, Shinpei reunited with the Ushio who was supposed to be dead. Unable to recall her own death, nor even the reason why she was there, Ushio innocently confessed her feelings to Shinpei.

## #010 -- Ushio's Smartphone

A smartphone received from the police officer Totsumura. He claimed Ushio handed it over to him the day before she died. It's locked with a password, so its contents cannot be viewed.

## #011 -- Ushio's Necklace

Made from a seashell that Shinpei gifted her back in elementary school. It seems she cherished it greatly, as it has been carefully maintained. Ushio was apparently wearing it right up until the accident.

## #012 -- Ushio's Ability ①

The abilities Ushio gained access to after recovering her memories are as follows:

Copying: The ability to scan and copy a target. • Physical Prowess: The incredible physical capabilities possessed by Shadows. • Hair Blades: The ability to weaponize her hair into sharp blades. • Memory Sharing: The ability to let others share and relive her own memories.

## #013 -- Ushio's Ability ②

When Shinpei loops, all events that occurred in the previous cycle are undone. However, Ushio alone is an exception; by looping alongside Shinpei, she retains her memories from previous cycles. It has become clear that Ushio can carry over not just memories, but also data scanned in previous cycles. This could be used to bring along weapons obtained in previous loops.

## #014 -- Uozumi Inn

We are a traditional inn boasting a 140-year history since the Meiji era. Located just a five-minute walk from the Hitogashima Island port, our inn sits upon a quiet hill overlooking the Kii Channel. Every room offers a panoramic view of the sea, and the breathtaking sunset seen from our open-air baths is sure to become a lifelong memory. Please enjoy our seasonal fish dishes, changing with the seasons.

## #015 -- Erika-chan

Real name unknown. 21 years old. Blood type A. Works part-time at Snack Miyako. She loves music and has a bit of a geeky streak. She is currently obsessed with 2.5D idols. She receives passionate advances from Totsumura, but she doesn't seem entirely opposed to it. Her snaggletooth is her charm point.

## #016 -- Trump Card

The trump card Hizuru had been hiding. Taking advantage of the fact that Shide's mud (his armor material) contains zero information, the plan is to have Ryunosuke possess that mud. Ryunosuke will print his own data onto the stolen mud to acquire a new body, while Shide, stripped of it, will lose his defenses.

## #017 -- Umi-okuri (Sea Send-off)

Hitogashima Island has a unique belief in castaway gods, stating that Hiruko-no-Mikoto resides within objects washed ashore and blesses them with bountiful catches. Umi-okuri (Sea Send-off) is a ritual held twice a year in summer and winter, where trash from the cleanup drive is carried on a shrine palanquin to the grounds, then burned to return it to the gods. It is said to bestow a blessing of good health upon the attendees.

## #018 -- Omoto Beach

A beautiful beach situated on the southern side of Hitogashima Island. It is beloved as a swimming spot. For those born and raised on the island, it is an extremely familiar location. Restaurant Kofune is built right beside the road that runs along Omoto Beach.

## #019 -- "I'll Protect You"

When Shinpei and Ushio were children, Ushio's prominent blonde hair and blue eyes made her a target for bullies. Worried about her, Shinpei promised, "I'll protect you," and gave her a seashell for her birthday. Ushio still cherishes that seashell to this day.

## #020 -- I Was Infected

The mysterious flash of light at Ushio's funeral. That flash was emitted when the Shadow Shiori copied Shinpei. Unbeknownst to him, Shinpei had been infected with Shadow Sickness since the very first loop.

## #021 -- Audio File

A mysterious audio file recorded on Hizuru Minakata's smartphone. It was recorded in the voice of a young woman with a Wakayama accent. Though there is a lot of static, the contents appear to be roughly as follows:

Something terrible is happening on Hitogashima Island. It involves Haine, and all the islanders will be killed at the Summer Festival on the 24th. The only person who can stop it is someone named "Shinpei Ajiro," but he can't do it alone. He needs help. Please come to Hitogashima Island and help "Shinpei."

## #022 -- Kaori Was Dragged Into It…

July 22nd, 9:00 AM. While lost in thought on the beach, Shinpei witnesses Shadow Mio murder and copy Totsumura, and is subsequently stabbed himself. Furthermore, Kaori, who happened to be passing by, is captured by Mio. Unable to process what was happening, Shinpei's consciousness faded to black.

## #025 -- Shadow's Ability ①

Shadows appear to possess the ability to spawn new Shadows. These born Shadows can apparently replicate not only the physical appearance but also the clothing and memories of the person they copy.

## #026 -- Shadow's Ability ②

A Shadow's physical prowess is far superior to a human's. They can move at speeds too fast for the eye to track, and possess the superhuman strength to easily lift an adult male with one hand. However, Shadows also have a weakness. Their true body is the "flat shadow" cast upon the ground. The three-dimensional form standing above the shadow is merely a lump of meat in the shape of a person; no matter how much you attack it, you cannot strike a decisive blow. But by inflicting a fatal wound on the flat true body, a Shadow can be eliminated. They despise having their flat form touched, and if you try to step on their shadow, the shadow itself will move to dodge.

## #027 -- Shadow's Ability ③

Even if a Shadow's three-dimensional body sustains damage, they can recover using incredible healing abilities. However, damage sustained by their flat true body cannot be healed. In such cases, the damage is projected onto their 3D form, seemingly accompanied by pain. Furthermore, these injuries appear impossible to treat with standard medical care such as disinfection or sutures.

## #028 -- Shadow's Ability ④

Shadows can copy objects to duplicate them. Not only can they print (transform) their own bodies, but for small items, they can even print them onto a plucked strand of hair. However, if they print something that consumes energy like electricity or gas, it only copies the shape and won't actually function. To make it function, the original copy source must be erased. The benefits of a functional copied item are immense; even if it runs out of electricity or bullets, the copy's owner can simply reprint it to restore it to full capacity.

## #029 -- Shadow's Ability ⑤

There are two types of copying. One is "transformation," where scanned information is projected onto one's own body, and the other is "parturition," where the body splits to birth a new Shadow.

The children birthed by Haine can give birth just once to multiply the Shadows, but the grandchildren birthed by those children lack the ability to give birth. Transformation, on the other hand, can be done endlessly by children and grandchildren alike. However, when copying a person, the original must be erased within a week, or the body will turn to mud and vanish.

## #030 -- Shadow Sickness

Something akin to an endemic disease passed down on Hitogashima Island. It is said that if you contract Shadow Sickness, you will start seeing a shadow that looks exactly like you, and those who see their shadow will be killed by it. Many people used to fall ill with it in the past, but it has rarely been heard of since the postwar era. It is said that those who fell ill were purified by Hiruko-sama.

## #031 -- Video of Shadow Mio

A video capturing the figure of Mio standing in front of Kofune at around 9:00 AM on July 22nd. The Mio in the video stands holding a kitchen knife, staring up at the second floor for about five minutes, before turning and walking off towards the mountains. Because the real Mio was taking a bath at this time, it is impossible for this to be the real her. It is precious footage proving the existence of Shadow Mio.

## #032 -- Interference with the Past

Shinpei's desperate feelings reached Ryunosuke's ears. Just as Haine reached out from her memories to leave a handprint on the present Shinpei, it might be possible for Shinpei to do something similar.

## #033 -- Gasoline

A type of petroleum product. Used in various applications as a liquid fuel. There are different types depending on its use and manufacturing method. It is highly volatile and burns violently even with an extremely small ignition source like static electricity, so mishandling it runs the risk of a major accident.

## #034 -- Capgras syndrome

A delusion in which one misidentifies a friend, spouse, parent, or other close relative as having been replaced by an identical-looking impostor. It can sometimes be caused by dementia or a head injury. Records remain of a mass outbreak of this on Hitogashima Island in the past.

## #035 -- Karikiri's Spare

Karikiri has survived for over 300 years by switching bodies. In case he died or suffered severe injuries from an accident or illness, he preserved his own past bodies, but they were destroyed in the war. However, there's a high probability that the body of his predecessor, Iwao Karikiri, who died after the war, is still preserved somewhere, and using it as a spare would explain why there were two Shides. Though how exactly he operated it remains unclear.

## #036 -- Masahito Karikiri ①

The chief priest of the Hitogashima Shrine. The head of the Karikiri family, who manages the island's rituals. He is very talkative, and if left alone, he will keep chatting endlessly. He also has a gamer side to him, sometimes traveling to Tokyo purely for gaming events. He apparently attended a university in Tokyo during his student days.

## #037 -- Masahito Karikiri ②

During the Edo period, Shidehiko Hishigata copulated with the god Haine and made her give birth. Perhaps due to the nature of Shadows, the child born grew to look exactly like Shidehiko. Seeing this, Shidehiko schemed to use Haine's power to transplant his memories into his son and rejuvenate himself. He has continued to make her give birth to him, surviving for 300 years— that is the true nature of Masahito Karikiri and Shide.

## #039 -- The Observer's Power

Shinpei's right eye views the flow of time from a high-dimensional perspective, observing countless parallel worlds. The world that Shinpei observes is then established as objective reality. Even if someone achieves something in a parallel world, it is a magnificent power that renders everything outside the world Shinpei observed (the one he inhabits) as if it never happened. However, Shinpei himself has not completely mastered this power, and cannot activate it unless he uses "death" as a trigger. That is the reason he was looping every time he died. It seems even Haine cannot steal the right eye from Shinpei.

## #040 -- Keychain

A keychain of the popular character "Egg Gentleman Eas-ta~kun". Since the first wave of merchandise was released in the early 2000s, the Egg Gentleman series has garnered support across a wide range of ages. Kaori has loved this character since she was a child, and Shinpei remembers her collecting Egg Gentleman goods for things like pencil boards and pencil cases.

## #041 -- Deja Vu

The feeling that one has previously experienced a situation that is in fact entirely new. Sometimes associated with memories from a past life.

## #042 -- Spider Form

Just before 8:00 AM on July 22nd. The mud enveloping Shide absorbed nearby Shadows and morphed into the shape of a giant spider. With a wider field of vision and reach than his humanoid form, Shinpei is tormented by movements unimaginably swift for a creature of that size. The plan failed, and Shide's sharp arm impaled Shinpei.

## #043 -- Game Playboy

A well-established monthly gaming magazine. Boasting a history of over 30 years, its price has remained fixed at 290 yen since its inaugural issue. Under the slogan "Making games 100 times more fun," they specialize in the latest game news, unflinchingly honest reviews, and reporting from a unique perspective. Totsumura is reading a back issue from June, which features the articles "10 Indie Games You Must Play Now" and "Infiltration: Gaming Salons in the Asia-Pacific Region!" The opening gravure spread features the rising idol Kaori Koyuba. Koba Mart stocks two copies every month, but because they sell out the moment they hit the shelves, the children of Hitogashima Island don't know this magazine exists.

## #044 -- The Sewers

There has always been a vast space running beneath Hitogashima Island. Combined with countless underground passageways and sewers dug by the military, it has morphed into an extremely complex maze. No one knows its full layout anymore. It is believed the Shadows utilized the sewers and such when moving around unseen.

## #045 -- Koba Mart

The only supermarket on Hitogashima Island. Its predecessor was the Kobayakawa Store, which operated since before the war. Opens at 8:00 AM. The owner is Tatsuo Kobayakawa. His wife Asako, whom he married 10 years ago, is a licensed pharmacist, allowing them to sell everything from daily necessities to medicines. Koba Mart is inextricably linked to the daily lives of the islanders.

## #046 -- The Kobayakawa Family Disappearance

July 23rd, 8:00 AM. Even though it was opening time, Koba Mart's shutters remained closed. When the neighbor, Shiomi-san, grew suspicious and peeked into the main house, it was completely empty. With no signs of having left the island by ferry, the three members of the Kobayakawa family vanished into thin air overnight.

## #047 -- Shiori Kobayakawa ①

A third-grade elementary school student. The daughter of Tatsuo and Asako Kobayakawa, who run Hitogashima Island's only supermarket, "Koba Mart." She is a bit precocious and strong-willed. Her friends tease her because she is a poor swimmer. On July 21st, she began to drown in the sea, but her life was saved by Ushio's desperate rescue efforts. However, the shock of losing the Ushio who saved her left her unable to speak.

## #048 -- Shiori Kobayakawa ②

Shiori Kobayakawa was a Shadow. Something flashed at Ushio's funeral venue during the 1st and 2nd loops, but nothing flashed in the 3rd loop. The reason for the difference was because Shinpei told her about Shadows, putting her on guard. The flash in the 1st and 2nd loops is believed to be from a copy taking place, but it is unknown who at the funeral was targeted.

## #049 -- Alain Kofune

Born in 1959, 59 years old. Originally from France. When he was 32, he met and fell in love with Kotoko, then 20, who had come to study abroad from Japan. Three years later, he immigrated to Hitogashima Island, married Kotoko, and opened Restaurant Kofune. Kotoko never fully recovered after giving birth to their second daughter Mio and passed away, but he lives a happy life surrounded by his biological daughters Ushio and Mio, and his foster son Shinpei.

## #050 -- Ushio Kofune

Born in 2000, 17 years old. Blood type A. A high school senior attending a prefectural high school on the mainland. Born to her French father Alain and Japanese mother Kotoko. She has blonde hair like her father. She is extremely strong-willed, but in elementary school, she was sometimes teased for her different appearance. She loves baking, and her specialties are cookies and chiffon cake. She absolutely hates supernatural phenomena. She was really looking forward to the Summer Festival, but lost her life in a marine accident on July 21st.

## #051 -- Mio Kofune

Born October 20, 2002. 15 years old. Blood type A. Ushio Kofune's biological younger sister. Unlike her sister, she takes after her mother with black hair. It is said her sun-tanned appearance looks exactly like her mother in her youth. A high school freshman attending the same prefectural high school as Ushio. Excellent athletic reflexes. She has won medals in swim meets. She loves eating, but is terrible at cooking. She considers her sister Ushio the most beautiful girl in the world, but believes herself to be her sister's complete opposite. "She acts just like Alain in how much she loves him and how she hates to lose," according to Shinpei.

## #052 -- Kaori Koyuba ①

Born November 2, 2000. 17 years old. Blood type A. A former classmate during their days at the Hitogashima Island Elementary and Middle School. She currently lives in Tokyo, but lived on Hitogashima Island until the 5th grade. She was shy and withdrawn, and often spent her time reading books in the library. She loves a character named "Egg Gentleman Eas-ta~kun."

## #053 -- Kaori Koyuba ②

One of the top idols of the present day. Boasting high vocal and acting abilities, she is widely loved across generations for her polite and approachable personality, and the occasional glimpses of an airhead. At the 2017 Red and White Song Battle, the group she belongs to performed as the closing act. She loves her hometown of Hitogashima Island, and never misses out on her hometown tax donations each year.

## #054 -- Koyuba Channel

The official channel opened by Kaori Koyuba on a video streaming site. Aside from the latest info on new songs and live concerts, it is also utilized for interacting with her fans. Perhaps owing to Kaori's personality and her attitude towards cherishing her fans, her live streams see virtually no troll comments.

## #055 -- Broken Brakes

The reason Mio fell into the sea was because her bicycle's brakes were broken. There were marks resembling a cut made into the wire; if it was a prank, it was a highly dangerous and malicious one.

## #056 -- The Night of the Tragedy

July 24th, just past 8:30 PM. The Hitogashima Shrine live concert venue, bustling with excitement from Kaori's appearance, turned into a hellscape. A four-armed monster that suddenly appeared lead an army of Shadows to slaughter the audience one after another. Shinpei desperately fought back, but was helpless and killed. Is there truly no way to stop that tragedy?

## #057 -- Shizuka Shiomi

The woman living next door to Koba Mart. She has known Asako Kobayakawa, the owner's wife, for a long time since they were children. She is talkative and can speak intimately with anyone. Her neighborhood gossip network surpasses the internet, making her the most well-informed person regarding the island's human relations.

## #058 -- Project Results Report

A report on the results of the tourism business on Hitogashima Island. It details trends in tourist numbers and tourist spending. It highlights that tourist numbers are slightly decreasing year over year, that the ratio of individual tourists exceeds that of tour groups, and that awareness among younger demographics is low. Proposed improvements include "improving awareness," "improving transportation convenience," "increasing lodging facilities," "attracting settings for movies/anime," and "applying for subsidies."

## #059 -- Shide

Haine's guardian. A massive four-armed monster. Nothing else is known about him yet besides the name Shide.

## #060 -- Stain Left on the Ground

With just a touch from Shadow Totsumura, Totsumura's corpse vanished without a trace, leaving nothing but a stain on the ground where it had been.

## #061 -- Incendiary Bomb

A type of bomb designed to start fires. Filled with oil, they burn the entire surrounding area upon impact. They were used during the war to burn down the wooden houses that were common in Japan at the time.

## #062 -- Corrosive Sublimate

Mercuric chloride. A deadly poison. Its handling is strictly restricted by the Poisonous and Deleterious Substances Control Law, but it used to be employed for disinfection and sterilization. Direct contact alone can cause dermatitis or nervous system abnormalities, and it is so highly toxic that drinking even a single drop leads to death.

## #063 -- Shinja Marsh

A pond located in the sacred forest of Hitogashima Shrine. The surrounding area forms a wetland, and it has been designated as a prefectural natural monument. Legend has it that En no Gyoja sealed a giant serpent here. Long ago, a massive serpent ran rampant, tormenting the people of Awaji Island and the mainland. Upon hearing this, En no Ozunu cornered the serpent using a sword granted by the gods. When the serpent begged for its life, promising never to do evil again, En no Ozunu forgave it and sealed it within this land.

## #064 -- Shinpei's Parents ①

His father is Toru, and his mother is Akemi. Both are underwater archaeologists. An underwater archaeologist is an expert equipped with the skills to survey underwater, and their targets range widely from sunken ships to submerged ruins. Neither was a native of Hitogashima Island, but they loved the island, overflowing with rich nature and history, and lived happy days there. However, 10 years ago in 2008, their boat sank during an accident at work, and they never returned.

## #065 -- Shinpei's Parents ②

A secret medical record was hidden in the Hishigata Clinic. The diagnoses were all listed as Capgras syndrome, but it was actually a registry of those who became food for Haine. The names of Toru and Akemi Ajiro, who supposedly died in a boating accident, were also there. After discovering the Hiruko Cave during a survey in the waters near Hitogashima Island, the two tried to announce it, but were killed by Shide to keep them quiet.

## #066 -- Reason Not to Kill Shinpei

July 24th. Shadow Shinpei, who appeared on the night of the festival, read Shinpei's memories and realized his looping ability. The Shadows seemingly have a grand objective, and Shadow Mio attempts to kill Shinpei, seeing him as an obstacle, but is stopped by Shadow Shinpei. Shadow Shinpei said: "If we let Shinpei die, we might just return to the 22nd again. Therefore, he must not be killed until everything is finished."

## #067 -- The Person I Love

Mio mustered her courage and confessed her feelings, but Shinpei says the one he loves is Ushio. Even though she already knew, hearing it said clearly was a huge shock. But still, Mio is Shinpei's precious little sister. Even if they can't be as close as lovers, they won't drift any further apart than they are now. Mio feels it was better to have bravely spoken up than to live carrying around those clouded feelings.

## #068 -- Snack Miyako

A snack bar located in the central town close to the Hitogashima Island port. It operates as a cafe during the day and is well-known even among the children on the island. The cafe hours are 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and the night sessions are from 7:00 PM to midnight. Because Totsumura, the island's resident officer, is a regular, frequent patrons sometimes call it the "Miyako Police Box." Totsumura himself is secretly nicknamed "T-800" by some of the staff, derived from the fact that the bottle-keep liquor he always orders costs 800 yen.

## #069 -- Champion of Justice

Totsumura's father was an excellent police officer. Protecting the weak and never yielding to evil, his father's figure looked like a superhero in the eyes of a young Totsumura. Totsumura joined the force wanting to become a police officer and fight for justice, but peaceful duty on Hitogashima Island differed greatly from his imagined days. Compounded by his innate laziness, he kept failing his promotion exams, leading to his current situation. Even so, the flame of justice burning in the depths of his heart has never been extinguished.

## #070 -- Time Capsule

A time capsule buried by everyone at school about 7 years ago, when Kaori moved away. Not just Shinpei and Kaori, but students from other grades also participated. Various things were stuffed inside, including toys, notebooks, and letters.

## #071 -- Mt. Takanosu

The mountainous area in the western part of the island. Wartime historical sites remain here, such as the ruins of the first and third gun batteries, and the searchlight post. It boasts the highest elevation on the island. On the west coast lies the Hitogashima Island Lighthouse and the ruins of the second gun battery.

## #072 -- Tokiko's a Shadow!?

July 22nd, just before 8:00 AM. Having shared his memories with his comrades through pseudo-looping, Shinpei and the others gathered at the school to intercept Haine and her forces. Ushio tries to stop Shinpei as he goes to greet Tokiko, who arrived late. However, she didn't make it in the nick of time, and Shinpei was shot to death by Tokiko. Tokiko was a Shadow. Unable to process it, Shinpei's consciousness faded.

## #073 -- Tokiko's Anger

July 22nd, 8:00 AM. Haine will betray the Hishigata family— Even conveying this truth, Tokiko turns a deaf ear. In the fierce battle, Sou takes a fatal wound while protecting Mio. Though shaken by having put Mio and Sou in danger, Tokiko deals the finishing blow to Shinpei with a ghastly expression. Was it out of desperation, or was it to redo everything? Perhaps her anger was directed at herself.

## #074 -- Unsolvable Code

This refers to "Tamam Shud," the cipher that appears in Ryunosuke Nagumo's work "Drift." It originates from a code held by an unidentified corpse discovered in Australia in the 1940s. It contains a string of incomprehensible alphabet letters, and what it represents remains unsolved to this day. In the story of "Drift," it appears in a way that allows for meta-level deduction.

## #075 -- Tokoyo

Haine's homeland, far beyond the distant sea. A mysterious world that living creatures of this realm cannot reach. The flow of time is stagnant there, making it an eternal, unchanging world.

## #076 -- doppelgänger

An entity that looks exactly like oneself. They have been spoken of since ancient times in legends and myths worldwide, and superstitions claiming that one who sees their doppelgänger will die are also well-known.

## #077 -- Tetsu Totsumura

A police officer working at the island's only police box, and a self-proclaimed "Champion of Justice." 34 years old. A relative of the Kofune family. His trademarks are his Western hat, sunglasses, and stubble. Browsing erotic websites is his daily routine. In this desperate situation, Shinpei narrowly escapes danger when Ushio cuts his throat to trigger a loop.

## #154 -- 6th END

July 22nd, 8:00 AM. All-out warfare is the only way to counter Haine and Shide. Shinpei decides to share Ushio's memories with his comrades, uniting their strength to intercept Haine. However, Shinpei's consciousness is suddenly cut short, and he is thrust back into the past once again.

## #155 -- 7th END

July 23rd, before 2:00 AM. Shide's true identity was Masahito Karikiri. Storming the Hitogashima Shrine, Shinpei demands to know why he killed his parents. He succeeds in defeating Karikiri, who does nothing but smile fearlessly, but Ushio is suddenly killed by another Karikiri appearing from behind.

## #156 -- 8th END

July 23rd, 11:00 AM. Through the machinations of Haine disguised as Shadow Shinpei, the group is split into two. Mio's group heads for the southwest fields, while Hizuru's group heads for Torashima (Tiger Island) in the northeast. After grouping up with Shadow Mio, Shinpei hurries toward the fields Mio headed for. However, Mio and the others have already been sent to the Hiruko Cave to serve as food for Mother. According to Tokiko, who managed to escape, it's an uphill battle over on Torashima as well. Shinpei resolves to loop. Swearing to execute the fastest possible rescue of both groups, he takes his own life.

## #157 -- 9th END

July 24th, before 6:00 AM. The Ushio he had been traveling with all this time was the Ushio from the 3rd loop. By having the 9th loop's Ushio read the memories she left behind, Shinpei and his allies magnificently resurrected Ushio. In order to outwit Haine and the others, Shinpei opted to loop from an advantageous position—what could his true intentions be?

## #158 -- route: Kaori Koyuba

Shinpei reunites with a young girl at the Hitogashima Island port. It's Kaori Koyuba, his classmate from elementary school. She says she was invited to perform in a live concert event at the Hitogashima Summer Festival. Shinpei is surprised that this girl, who gave off such a quiet and shy impression, has become a popular idol. Largely due to her appearance, this year's Summer Festival records unprecedented attendance, but behind the scenes, a terrifying plan is being devised.

## #159 -- route: Tetsu Totsumura

Feeling a sense of crisis regarding the existence of the Shadows, Shinpei and Mio rely on Totsumura to enlist the help of the police. Their feelings get through to a half-doubting Totsumura, who begins to burn with his mission as a Hero of Justice. However, with a detective from the mainland going missing immediately after arriving on the island, the demonic hands of the Shadows are steadily drawing closer. Is there truly a way to defeat the Shadows?

## #160 -- route: Mio Kofune

On the night of the Hitogashima Summer Festival, the shrine grounds are thrown into a panic by the sudden appearance of the Shadows. Shinpei grabs Mio's hand and flees. To keep his promise to Ushio, he cannot leave Mio all alone. He will protect her, even at the cost of his own life. Having resolved this, Shinpei regroups with Alain and considers escaping from the island, but is completely unaware that they have walked right into the enemy's trap.

## #161 -- route: Hizuru Minakata

Transported into the memories of Hizuru's middle school days, Shinpei and Ushio learn that Hizuru and Haine used to be close friends. Perhaps they can change history. Thinking this, Shinpei and Ushio succeed in interfering with past events. However, things don't go entirely as planned, and a tribulation different from the original history awaits them.

## #162 -- route: Tokiko Hishigata

Tokiko finds herself agonizing between her beloved family and Haine on one side, and her best friends like Mio on the other. She just wants to live a peaceful, happy life, but caught in relationships that cannot coexist, unable to consult anyone, only her sins and suffering continue to swell. At last, the time comes for her to make a decision. Was her choice the right one? Nobody knows.

## #163 -- route: Ushio Kofune

Shinpei returns to Hitogashima Island to attend Ushio's funeral. He and Ushio had an argument over his move to Tokyo, and hadn't made up since. Shinpei fiercely regrets that he postponed it when he had countless opportunities to make amends. Strange things begin happening around him. Eerie Shadows strolling the island, and the inexplicable phenomenon of returning to the past every time he is killed. And then, Shinpei reunites with the supposedly dead Ushio.

## #164 -- epilogue: Kaori Koyuba

Kaori is scheduled to share her memories of Hitogashima Island at a talk show. She hunts for material alongside Shinpei and the others, but they can't quite land on a highly impactful episode. Amidst this, Ushio remembers the time capsule. The one everyone buried when Kaori was transferring schools. It's a perfect anecdote about her hometown, and a golden opportunity to awaken her memories from that time. Relying on limited clues, the group begins their search for the time capsule. But Kaori seems dispirited. Inside there lies a secret she doesn't want anyone to see.

## #165 -- epilogue: Mio Kofune

Shinpei and Ushio reunite for the first time in two years. Even though they aren't officially dating, seeing them mutually yearn for each other leaves a cloud in Mio's heart. Shinpei treats her gently without noticing her feelings. What does she want to do? What should she do? To get rid of this cloud and take even a small step forward, Mio resolves to confess her feelings.

## #166 -- epilogue: Hizuru Minakata

Stuck on ideas for her new novel, Hizuru returns to Hitogashima Island seeking a way out of her slump. She intended to quietly brainstorm on her own, but ends up having to look after her niece, Hoine. Though initially bewildered, acting alongside Hoine makes Hizuru realize she is viewing the island from a different perspective than usual. Gathering the fragments of sparkling ideas left in their footprints, Hizuru weaves a new tale.

## #167 -- epilogue: Ushio Kofune

Shinpei and Ushio reunite for the first time in two years. Things had been awkward between them since Shinpei moved to Tokyo, but driven by terribly overwhelming affection and the fear of never seeing each other again, they instinctively embrace. Even though they continue to drift past each other afterward, they remain connected in their hearts, reaffirming that they are absolutely indispensable to one another.
