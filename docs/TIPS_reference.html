#000 -- Shinpei Ajiro

Click to reveal

Born May 8, 2000. 18 years old. Blood type O. He was born the eldest son of the Ajiro family of underwater archaeologists, but lost his parents in an accident at age 8. Alain Kofune became his foster father. Influenced by Alain and his daughter Ushio, he discovered a passion for cooking and moved to Tokyo after middle school. He currently attends a culinary school where he can also earn his high school diploma. His specialty is chicken curry. Influenced by his biological father, he has loved books since childhood, particularly mysteries and sci-fi. His favorite author is Ryunosuke Nagumo. He has a habit of taking a step back to view things objectively from a bird's-eye perspective. He is afraid of water and cannot swim.

#002 -- Amenonuhoko

Click to reveal

A sacred treasure of the Hitogashima Shrine. It shares its name with the spear used to create the land in Japanese mythology. According to the shrine's history, it has been passed down for over several millennia, and it is said that the drops from this spear birthed the island of Onogoro. The spearhead is forged from rust-proof meteoric iron, and while intended for ceremonial use, it possesses high lethal force. The handle portion has been remade numerous times over the years; the current one was crafted from oak during the Edo period.

#005 -- Event Horizon

Click to reveal

The observational boundary. It refers to a cliff-like concept that steadily closes in with each loop. It represents the limit of the scope where a do-over is possible on the timeline, creeping up behind Shinpei as time flows forward. The further away this cliff is, the more time he has to try again; the closer it gets, the less leeway he has left. If this cliff catches up to him and he falls, Shinpei's death will be finalized, and from then on, the timeline observed by Haine will become cemented as objective reality.

#026 -- Shadow's Ability ②

Click to reveal

A Shadow's physical prowess is far superior to a human's. They can move at speeds too fast for the eye to track, and possess the superhuman strength to easily lift an adult male with one hand. However, Shadows also have a weakness. Their true body is the "flat shadow" cast upon the ground. The three-dimensional form standing above the shadow is merely a lump of meat in the shape of a person; no matter how much you attack it, you cannot strike a decisive blow. But by inflicting a fatal wound on the flat true body, a Shadow can be eliminated. They despise having their flat form touched, and if you try to step on their shadow, the shadow itself will move to dodge.

#027 -- Shadow's Ability ③

Click to reveal

Even if a Shadow's three-dimensional body sustains damage, they can recover using incredible healing abilities. However, damage sustained by their flat true body cannot be healed. In such cases, the damage is projected onto their 3D form, seemingly accompanied by pain. Furthermore, these injuries appear impossible to treat with standard medical care such as disinfection or sutures.

↑ top

#028 -- Shadow's Ability ④

Click to reveal

Shadows can copy objects to duplicate them. Not only can they print (transform) their own bodies, but for small items, they can even print them onto a plucked strand of hair. However, if they print something that consumes energy like electricity or gas, it only copies the shape and won't actually function. To make it function, the original copy source must be erased. The benefits of a functional copied item are immense; even if it runs out of electricity or bullets, the copy's owner can simply reprint it to restore it to full capacity.

↑ top

#029 -- Shadow's Ability ⑤

Click to reveal

There are two types of copying. One is "transformation," where scanned information is projected onto one's own body, and the other is "parturition," where the body splits to birth a new Shadow.

The children birthed by Haine can give birth just once to multiply the Shadows, but the grandchildren birthed by those children lack the ability to give birth. Transformation, on the other hand, can be done endlessly by children and grandchildren alike. However, when copying a person, the original must be erased within a week, or the body will turn to mud and vanish.

When a fisherman's daughter approached, the whale's body shone blindingly bright, and in the blink of an eye, it transformed into an exact replica of the daughter. And then, it devoured her.
